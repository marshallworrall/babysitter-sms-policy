Babysitter Scheduling — Household SMS Notification Service

Private household automation tool — not a commercial service

What this is

This is a private, household-use automation tool operated by Marshall Worrall to coordinate babysitting coverage for our family. It is not a commercial product, marketing service, or business of any kind.

The tool monitors our daughter's school calendar for holidays and early-dismissal days, then sends SMS messages to a small, personally known list of babysitters to ask about their availability. All recipients are individuals known personally to our family who have given explicit verbal consent to receive these messages.

Who receives messages

Messages are sent only to a fixed private list of babysitters who have individually agreed to be contacted by SMS for scheduling purposes. No messages are sent to unknown recipients, purchased lists, or anyone who has not personally consented.

Messages are also sent to the two household members (myself and my wife) as booking confirmations and scheduling notifications.

Types of messages sent

All messages fall into one of these categories:


Availability request Booking confirmation Cancellation notice Schedule alert

Example messages:

"Hi Sarah, are you available to babysit on April 18 from 3:00pm to 7:00pm? Reply YES or NO to this number. Thanks!"

"Babysitter confirmed: Sarah on April 18, 3:00pm–7:00pm. Calendar updated."

Message frequency

Message volume is low — typically between 2 and 10 messages per month in total across all recipients, sent only when a childcare need arises. No recurring, promotional, or unsolicited messages are ever sent.

How recipients opted in

Each babysitter on our contact list has been asked personally — by phone call or in person — whether they are willing to receive SMS scheduling requests from our family. Only those who agreed are included. Consent is recorded privately and can be provided to carriers upon request.

How to opt out

Any recipient can stop receiving messages at any time by replying STOP to any message. Their number will be permanently removed from the contact list and no further messages will be sent.

Reply STOP to any message to unsubscribe immediately.

Privacy Policy

Babysitter Scheduling SMS Service — last updated April 9, 2026

Who we are

This privacy policy applies to the private household SMS scheduling service operated by Marshall Worrall. This is not a commercial service. It is a personal automation tool used solely to coordinate childcare within our household.

How recipients opt in

No one receives scheduling messages without first completing an explicit SMS opt-in. Before being added to the contact list, each recipient is sent an opt-in request from our messaging number asking them to reply YES to confirm consent. Only recipients who reply YES are added to the active contact list.

Opting in is entirely voluntary. Recipients who do not reply or who reply STOP are not contacted further.

Consent is confirmed by replying YES to the initial opt-in request. It can be withdrawn at any time by replying STOP to any message.

What information we collect

We collect and store only the minimum information needed to operate the service:

  • First name and mobile phone number of each recipient who has completed the SMS opt-in
  • Records of SMS opt-in consent — the message exchange in which a recipient replied YES to our opt-in request, retained as documentation of consent
  • Dates, times, and status of babysitting bookings
  • SMS reply content from babysitters (availability responses and cancellation notices)

We do not collect email addresses, payment information, location data, or any other personal information beyond what is listed above.

How we use this information

Information is used solely to:

  • Send babysitting availability requests via SMS
  • Send booking confirmations and cancellation notices
  • Document and retain opt-in consent records
  • Track scheduling history for our household

Your information is never sold, shared, rented, or disclosed to any third party for any purpose. It is never used for marketing, advertising, or any commercial purpose.

How we store information

Contact information and consent records are stored in a private configuration file on a personal computer within our household. They are not stored in any cloud database or shared server beyond what is required to send SMS messages via Twilio.

Opt-in consent exchanges are also retained in Twilio message logs and are available for carrier verification upon request.

Twilio's privacy policy governs the handling of message delivery data on their platform. You can review it at twilio.com/legal/privacy.

How to opt out

Any recipient can stop receiving messages at any time by replying STOP to any message. Their number will be permanently removed from the contact list and no further messages will be sent. Requests to remove personal information entirely can also be made by contacting us directly — contact details are on file with Twilio as part of the A2P 10DLC brand registration.

Contact

This service is operated by Marshall Worrall. Contact details are on file with Twilio as part of the A2P 10DLC brand registration.

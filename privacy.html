Privacy Policy

Babysitter Scheduling SMS Service — last updated April 2026

Who we are

This privacy policy applies to the private household SMS scheduling service operated by [Your Full Name]. This is not a commercial service. It is a personal automation tool used solely to coordinate childcare within our household.

What information we collect

We collect and store only the minimum information needed to operate the service:

  • First name and mobile phone number of each babysitter who has consented to receive messages
  • Dates, times, and status of babysitting bookings
  • SMS reply content from babysitters (yes/no responses and cancellation notices)

We do not collect email addresses, payment information, location data, or any other personal information beyond what is listed above.

How we use this information

Information is used solely to:

  • Send babysitting availability requests via SMS
  • Send booking confirmations and cancellation notices
  • Track scheduling history for our household

Your information is never sold, shared, rented, or disclosed to any third party for any purpose. It is never used for marketing, advertising, or any commercial purpose.

How we store information

Contact information is stored in a private configuration file on a personal computer within our household. It is not stored in any cloud database, shared server, or third-party service beyond what is required to send SMS messages via Twilio.

Twilio's privacy policy governs the handling of message delivery data on their platform. You can review it at twilio.com/legal/privacy.

How to opt out

Any recipient can stop receiving messages at any time by replying STOP to any message. Their number will be permanently removed and no further messages will be sent. Requests to remove personal information entirely can also be made by contacting us directly.

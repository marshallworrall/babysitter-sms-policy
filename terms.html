Program description

The Babysitter Scheduling SMS Service is a private household automation tool operated by [Your Full Name]. It sends SMS messages to a small, personally known list of babysitters to coordinate childcare scheduling for our family.

This is not a commercial service, subscription program, or marketing campaign of any kind.

Message types and frequency

Recipients may receive the following types of messages:

  • Babysitting availability requests
  • Booking confirmation notices
  • Cancellation or rescheduling notices
  • Schedule change alerts

Message frequency is low — typically 2 to 10 messages per month in total across all recipients, sent only when a scheduling need arises. No recurring, promotional, or unsolicited messages are sent.

Message and data rates

Standard message and data rates may apply depending on your mobile carrier plan. This service does not charge recipients any fees.

How to get help

Reply HELP to any message to receive information about this service. You can also contact the service operator directly — contact details are on file with Twilio as part of the A2P 10DLC brand registration.

How to opt out

You can stop receiving messages at any time. Two ways to opt out:

  • Reply STOP to any message — you will be removed immediately and receive no further messages
  • Contact the service operator directly to request removal

Reply STOP to unsubscribe immediately. Reply HELP for help.
